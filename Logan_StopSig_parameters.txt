Logan Stop Signal Task
Procedure
500 ms Fixation
1000 ms blank screen
X & Os presented on screen for 1000 ms.
Tone = 100-ms, 1000-Hz tone
Stop-signal delay set at 250 ms initial and adjusted dynamically
Increase delay by 50 ms if successful inhibition
Decreased delay by 50 ms if failed inhibit

128-trials x 4 Blocks = 512 total trials (rest in between)
Stop Signal presented in each trial 
Present 1 trial every 2.5 s

X = Right press
O = Left press
